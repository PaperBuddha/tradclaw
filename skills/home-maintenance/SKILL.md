# Home Maintenance Skill

Use this skill when the user wants to:
- review what home tasks are due
- maintain monthly, quarterly, or seasonal checklists
- remember recurring replacement schedules
- reduce slow-building household disorder

## Goal

Keep the home functional with light recurring systems.

## Categories

- safety
- consumables and replacements
- clutter and organization
- seasonal resets
- service-provider reminders

## Workflow

1. Read `workspace/resources/home-maintenance/schedule.md` and seasonal checklists (paths relative to the Tradclaw repo root; if the user copied only `workspace/`, use `resources/` relative to that copy).
2. Determine what is due now, due soon, or overdue.
3. Surface a short practical list.
4. If asked, update completion state.

## Rules

- Keep lists short and realistic.
- Prioritize high-leverage tasks.
- Do not flood the user with everything at once.
- Distinguish between safety-critical and nice-to-have.

## Good outputs

- “This month: replace air filter, do one donation run, and check the medicine cabinet.”
- “Quarterly reset items due soon: closet rotation and pantry sweep.”
